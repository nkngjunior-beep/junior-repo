About Us

BTech Virtual Card is a web-based simulation platform designed to help students, professionals, and developers understand the core features of digital banking using virtual prepaid cards.

Our mission is to provide a secure, educational experience that allows users to load, spend, and track their virtual cards using simulated currencies (XAF, USD, EUR).
